“Once upon a time there were three little sisters,” the Dormouse began in a great hurry, “and their names were Elsie, Lacie, and Tillie; and they live
d at the bottom of a well—”

“What did they live on?”
I asked, who always took a great interest in questions of eating and drinking.
The Dormouse, after thinking a minute or two, said, “They lived on treacle.”
