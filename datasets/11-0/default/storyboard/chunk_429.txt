“Wake up, Alice dear!” said my sister; “Why, what a long sleep you’ve had!”

“Oh, I’ve had such a curious dream!”
I said, and I told my sister, as well as I could remember them, all these strange Adventures of mine that you have just been reading about; and when I
had finished, my sister kissed me, and said, “It _was_ a curious dream, dear, certainly: but now run in to your tea; it’s getting late.”
