An old Crab took the opportunity of saying to her daughter, “Ah, my dear!
Let this be a lesson to you never to lose _your_ temper!”

“Hold your tongue, Ma!” said the young Crab, a little snappishly.
“You’re enough to try the patience of an oyster!”

“I wish I had our Dinah here, I know I do!”
I said aloud, addressing nobody in particular.
