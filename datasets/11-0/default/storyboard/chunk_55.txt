The Mouse looked at me rather inquisitively, and seemed to me to wink with one of its little eyes, but it said nothing.
“Perhaps it doesn’t understand English,” I thought; “I daresay it’s a French mouse, come over with William the Conqueror.”
(For, with all my knowledge of history, I had no very clear notion how long ago anything had happened.)
So I began again: “Où est ma chatte?” which was the first sentence in my French lesson-book.
