Taking courage, I continued, “I didn’t know that Cheshire cats always grinned; in fact, I didn’t know that cats _could_ grin.”
“They all can,” the Duchess said, “and most of ’em do.”

“I don’t know of any that do,” I said politely, feeling pleased to have started a conversatio
n.
“You don’t know much,” the Duchess retorted, “and that’s a fact.”
