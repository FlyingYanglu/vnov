“As wet as ever,” I said in a melancholy tone: “it doesn’t seem to dry me at all.”

“In that case,” said the Dodo solemnly, rising to its feet, “I mov
e that the meeting adjourn, for the immediate adoption of more energetic remedies—”

“Speak English!” said the Eaglet.
“I don’t know the meaning of half those long words, and, what’s more, I don’t believe you do either!”
And the Eaglet bent down its head to hide a smile: some of the other birds tittered audibly.
