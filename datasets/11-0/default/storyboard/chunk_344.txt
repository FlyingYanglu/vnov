“Oh, as to the whiting,” the Mock Turtle said, “they—you’ve seen them, of course?”
I nodded, remembering the times I had seen them at dinner.
“Yes,” I replied, “I’ve often seen them at dinn—” I quickly stopped myself, realizing I might have said too much.
“I don’t know where Dinn may be,” the Mock Turtle said, “but if you’ve seen them so often, of course you know what they’re like.”
