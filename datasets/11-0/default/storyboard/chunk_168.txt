“Whoever lives there,” I thought, “it’ll never do to come upon them _this_ size: why, I should frighten them out of their wits!”
So I began nibbling at the righthand bit again, and did not venture to go near the house till I had brought myself down to nine inches high.
I stood there, staring at the quaint little house, trying to figure out my next move.
Suddenly, a footman in livery came rushing out of the woods.
