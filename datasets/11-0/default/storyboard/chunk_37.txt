It was as much as I could do, lying down on one side, to look through into the garden with one eye; but to get through was more hopeless than ever.
“You ought to be ashamed of yourself,” I said to myself, “a great girl like you, to go on crying in this way!
I sat down and began to cry again. Stop this moment, I tell you!”
