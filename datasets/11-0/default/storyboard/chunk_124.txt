After a minute or two, they began moving about again, and I heard the Rabbit say, “A barrowful will do, to begin with.”
I thought; but I had not long to doubt, for the next moment a shower of little pebbles came rattling in at the window, and some of them hit her in the
face.
“I’ll put a stop to this,” I said to myself, and shouted out, “You’d better not do that again!” which produced another dead silence.
