“What I was going to say,” said the Dodo in an offended tone, “was, that the best thing to get us dry would be a Caucus-race.”
I said; not that I wanted much to know, but the Dodo had paused as if it thought that _somebody_ ought to.
“What _is_ a Caucus-race?” “Speak English!” said the Eaglet.
“I don’t know the meaning of half those long words, and, what’s more, I don’t believe you do either!”
