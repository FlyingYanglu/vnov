I heard the sound of many footsteps and looked around, eager to see the Queen.
First came ten soldiers carrying clubs; they were all shaped like the three gardeners, oblong and flat, with their hands and feet at the corners.
Next, the ten courtiers followed; they were ornamented all over with diamonds and walked two and two, as the soldiers did.
After them came the royal children; there were ten of them, and they were all jumping merrily along hand in hand, in couples, and were all ornamented
