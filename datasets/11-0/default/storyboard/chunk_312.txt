“Let’s go on with the game,” the Queen said to me; and I was too much frightened to say a word, but slowly followed her back to the croquet-ground.
The other guests had taken advantage of the Queen’s absence, and were resting in the shade: however, the moment they saw her, they hurried back to the
game, the Queen merely remarking that a moment’s delay would cost them their lives.
All the time they were playing, the Queen never left off quarrelling with the other players, and shouting “Off with his head!”
