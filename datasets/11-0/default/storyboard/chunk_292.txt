By the time I had caught the flamingo and brought it back, the fight was over, and both the hedgehogs were out of sight: “but it doesn’t matter much,”
I thought, “as all the arches are gone from this side of the ground.”
So I tucked it away under my arm, that it might not escape again, and went back for a little more conversation with my friend.
