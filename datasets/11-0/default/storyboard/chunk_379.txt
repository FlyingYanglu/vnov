This of course, I could _not_ stand, and I went round the court and got behind him, and very soon found an opportunity of taking it away.
I did it so quickly that the poor little juror (it was Bill, the Lizard) could not make out at all what had become of it; so, after hunting all about
for it, he was obliged to write with one finger for the rest of the day; and this was of very little use, as it left no mark on the slate.
“Herald, read the accusation!” said the King.
