“Would _you_ like cats if you were me?”

“Well, perhaps not,” I said in a soothing tone: “don’t be angry about it.
And yet I wish I could show you our cat Dinah: I think you’d take a fancy to cats if you could only see her.
She is such a dear quiet thing,” I went on, half to myself, as I swam lazily about in the pool, “and she sits purring so nicely by the fire, licking h
er paws and washing her face—and she is such a nice soft thing to nurse—and she’s such a capital one for catching mice—oh, I beg your pardon!”
