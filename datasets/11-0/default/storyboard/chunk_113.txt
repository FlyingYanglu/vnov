Why, it fills the whole window!” the Rabbit retorted angrily.
“Sure, it does, yer honour: but it’s an arm for all that.”
“Well, it’s got no business there, at any rate: go and take it away!”
There was a long silence after this, and I could only hear whispers now and then; such as, “Sure, I don’t like it, yer honour, at all, at all!”
