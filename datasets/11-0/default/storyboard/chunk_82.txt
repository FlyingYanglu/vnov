“Mine is a long and a sad tale!” said the Mouse, turning to me and sighing.
“It _is_ a long tail, certainly,” I said, looking down with wonder at the Mouse’s tail; “but why do you call it sad?”
And I kept on puzzling about it while the Mouse was speaking, so that my idea of the tale was something like this:—
