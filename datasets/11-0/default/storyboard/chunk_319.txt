We had not gone far before we saw the Mock Turtle in the distance, sitting sad and lonely on a little ledge of rock.
As we came nearer, I could hear him sighing as if his heart would break.
I pitied him deeply. "What is his sorrow?"
The Gryphon answered, very nearly in the same words as before, "It’s all his fancy, that: he hasn’t got no sorrow, you know.
