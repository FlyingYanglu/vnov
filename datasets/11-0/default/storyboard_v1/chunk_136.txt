I asked, who always took a great interest in questions of eating and drinking.
 The Dormouse, after thinking a minute or two, said, “They lived on treacle.”
“They couldn’t have done that, you know,”
 I gently remarked, “they’d have been ill.”
 “So they were,”
 the Dormouse said, “_very_ ill.”
I tried to fancy to myself what such an extraordinary way of living would be like, but it puzzled me too much, so I went on, “But why did they live at the bottom of a well?”
