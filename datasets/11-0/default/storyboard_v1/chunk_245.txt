The Dormouse was hastily removed, and by the time things settled down, the cook had mysteriously vanished.
The King sighed in relief, “Never mind! Call the next witness.”
 He leaned towards the Queen and whispered, “Really, my dear, you must cross-examine the next witness. It quite makes my forehead ache!”
 I watched the White Rabbit fumble with his list, feeling a growing curiosity about who the next witness would be.
 “—for they haven’t got much evidence yet,”
 I thought to myself.
 My heart skipped a beat when the White Rabbit, in his shrill little voice, read out the name “Alice!”
I barely had time to process what was happening when I jumped up, forgetting how large I had become.
