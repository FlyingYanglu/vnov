 Some of the jury wrote it down "important,"
 and some "unimportant."
 I could see this, as I was near enough to look over their slates;
 "but it doesn’t matter a bit,"
 I thought to myself.
At this moment, the King, who had been for some time busily writing in his note-book, cackled out "Silence!"
