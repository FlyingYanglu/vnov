"Not I!"
 he replied.
 "We quarrelled last March—just before _he_ went mad, you know—"
 (pointing with his tea spoon at the March Hare,) "—it was at the great concert given by the Queen of Hearts, and I had to sing 'Twinkle, twinkle, little bat! How I wonder what you’re at!' You know the song, perhaps?"
 "I’ve heard something like it,"
 I said.
 "It goes on, you know,"
 the Hatter continued, "in this way:— 'Up above the world you fly, Like a tea-tray in the sky. Twinkle, twinkle—'"
