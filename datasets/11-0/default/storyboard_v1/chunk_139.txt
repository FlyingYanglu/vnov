“I’ve had nothing yet,”
 I replied in an offended tone, “so I can’t take more.”
 The Hatter said, “You mean you can’t take _less_. It’s very easy to take _more_ than nothing.”
“Nobody asked _your_ opinion,”
 I said.
 The Hatter asked triumphantly, “Who’s making personal remarks now?”
I did not quite know what to say to this, so I helped myself to some tea and bread-and-butter, and then turned to the Dormouse, and repeated my question, “Why did they live at the bottom of a well?”
The Dormouse again took a minute or two to think about it, and then said, “It was a treacle-well.”
