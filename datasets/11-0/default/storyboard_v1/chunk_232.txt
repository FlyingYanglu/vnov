“I keep them to sell,”
 the Hatter added as an explanation;
 “I’ve noneof my own. I’m a hatter.”
Here the Queen put on her spectacles, and began staring at the Hatter,who turned pale and fidgeted.
“Give your evidence,”
 said the King;
 “and don’t be nervous, or I’llhave you executed on the spot.”
This did not seem to encourage the witness at all: he kept shiftingfrom one foot to the other, looking uneasily at the Queen, and in hisconfusion he bit a large piece out of his teacup instead of thebread-and-butter.
