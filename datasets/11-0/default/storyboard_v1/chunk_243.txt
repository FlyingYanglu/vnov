“Give your evidence,”
 the King said.
“Shan’t,”
 the cook replied.
The King looked anxiously at the White Rabbit, who said in a low voice, “Your Majesty must cross-examine _this_ witness.”
“Well, if I must, I must,”
 the King said, with a melancholy air.
 He folded his arms and frowned at the cook till his eyes were nearly out of sight.
