“Talking of axes,”
 the Duchess interrupted, “chop off her head!”
I glanced anxiously at the cook, wondering if she would take the hint.
 But the cook was busy stirring the soup and didn’t seem to be listening.
 So I continued, “Twenty-four hours, I _think_; or is it twelve? I—”
“Oh, don’t bother _me_,”
 the Duchess said impatiently.
 “I never could abide figures!”
