“An arm, you goose! Who ever saw one that size? Why, it fills the whole window!”
 the Rabbit retorted angrily.
“Sure, it does, yer honour: but it’s an arm for all that.”
“Well, it’s got no business there, at any rate: go and take it away!”
There was a long silence after this, and I could only hear whispers now and then;
 such as, “Sure, I don’t like it, yer honour, at all, at all!”
 “Do as I tell you, you coward!”
 and at last I spread out my hand again, and made another snatch in the air.
