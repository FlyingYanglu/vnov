 And then, turning to the rose-tree, she went on, “What _have_ you been doing here?”
“May it please your Majesty,”
 said Two, in a very humble tone, going down on one knee as he spoke, “we were trying—”
“_I_ see!”
 said the Queen, who had meanwhile been examining the roses.
 “Off with their heads!”
