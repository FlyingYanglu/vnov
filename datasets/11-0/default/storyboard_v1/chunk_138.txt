The Dormouse again took a minute or two to think about it, and then said, “It was a treacle-well.”
“There’s no such thing!”
 I was beginning very angrily, but the Hatter and the March Hare went “Sh! sh!”
 and the Dormouse sulkily remarked, “If you can’t be civil, you’d better finish the story for yourself.”
“No, please go on!”
 I said very humbly, “I won’t interrupt again. I dare say there may be _one_.”
“Take some more tea,”
 the March Hare said to me, very earnestly.
