“You may go,”
 the King said, and the Hatter hurriedly left the court, without even waiting to put his shoes on.
“—and just take his head off outside,”
 the Queen added to one of the officers, but the Hatter was out of sight before the officer could get to the door.
---“Call the next witness!”
 the King ordered.
The next witness was the Duchess’s cook.
 She carried the pepper-box in her hand, and I guessed who it was even before she got into the court, by the way the people near the door began sneezing all at once.
