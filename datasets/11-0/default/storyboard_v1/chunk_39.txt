” and, when it had finished this short speech, they all cheered.I thought the whole thing very absurd, but they all looked so grave that I did not dare to laugh; and, as I could not think of anything to say, I simply bowed, and took the thimble, looking as solemn as I could.The next thing was to eat the comfits: this caused some noise and confusion, as the large birds complained that they could not taste theirs, and the small ones choked and had to be patted on the back. However, it was over at last, and they sat down again in a ring, and begged the Mouse to tell them something more.“
