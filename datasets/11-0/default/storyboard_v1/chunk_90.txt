 He was still gazing at the sky, which I found decidedly uncivil.
 “But perhaps he can’t help it,”
 I thought to myself.
 “His eyes are so very nearly at the top of his head. But at any rate he might answer questions.—How am I to get in?”
 I repeated aloud.
“I shall sit here,”
 the Frog-Footman remarked, “till tomorrow—”
