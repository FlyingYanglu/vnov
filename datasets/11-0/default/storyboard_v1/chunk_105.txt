“Cheshire Puss,”
 I began, rather timidly, as I did not at all know whether it would like the name: however, it only grinned a little wider.
 “Come, it’s pleased so far,”
 I thought, and I went on.
 “Would you tell me, please, which way I ought to go from here?”
“That depends a good deal on where you want to get to,”
 said the Cat.
“I don’t much care where—”
