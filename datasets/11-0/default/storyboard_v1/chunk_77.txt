I said: “three inches is such a wretched height to be.”
“It is a very good height indeed!”
 said the Caterpillar angrily, rearing itself upright as it spoke (it was exactly three inches high).
 “But I’m not used to it!”
 I pleaded in a piteous tone.
 And I thought to myself, “I wish the creatures wouldn’t be so easily offended!”
“You’ll get used to it in time,”
 said the Caterpillar;
