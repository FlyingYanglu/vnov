 I said to myself, "in my going out altogether, like a candle. I wonder what I should be like then?"
 And I tried to fancy what the flame of a candle is like after the candle is blown out, for I could not remember ever having seen such a thing.
After a while, finding that nothing more happened, I decided on going into the garden at once.
 But, alas for poor Alice!
 When I got to the door, I found I had forgotten the little golden key.
 When I went back to the table for it, I found I could not possibly reach.
“What a curious feeling!”
