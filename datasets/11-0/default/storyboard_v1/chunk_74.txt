“You are old,”
 said the youth, “one would hardly suppose That your eye was as steady as ever; Yet you balanced an eel on the end of your nose— What made you so awfully clever?”
“I have answered three questions, and that is enough,”
 Said his father;
 “don’t give yourself airs! Do you think I can listen all day to such stuff? Be off, or I’ll kick you down stairs!”
“That is not said right,”
 said the Caterpillar.
