Nobody moved.
“Who cares for you?”
 I said, (I had grown to my full size by this time.) “You’re nothing but a pack of cards!”
At this the whole pack rose up into the air, and came flying down upon me: I gave a little scream, half of fright and half of anger, and tried to beat them off, and found myself lying on the bank, with my head in the lap of my sister, who was gently brushing away some dead leaves that had fluttered down from the trees upon my face.
“Wake up, Alice dear!”
 said my sister;
 “Why, what a long sleep you’ve had!”
“Oh, I’ve had such a curious dream!”
