"You might just as well say that ‘I see what I eat’ is the same thing as ‘I eat what I see’!"
 "You might just as well say,"
 added the March Hare, "that ‘I like what I get’ is the same thing as ‘I get what I like’!"
 "You might just as well say,"
 added the Dormouse, who seemed to be talking in his sleep, "that ‘I breathe when I sleep’ is the same thing as ‘I sleep when I breathe’!"
 "It is the same thing with you,"
 said the Hatter, and here the conversation dropped.
 The party sat silent for a minute while I thought over all I could remember about ravens and writing-desks, which wasn’t much.
