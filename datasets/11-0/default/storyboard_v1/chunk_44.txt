said the young Crab, a little snappishly.
 “You’re enough to try the patience of an oyster!”
“I wish I had our Dinah here, I know I do!”
 I said aloud, addressing nobody in particular.
 “She’d soon fetch it back!”
“And who is Dinah, if I might venture to ask the question?”
 said the Lory.
I replied eagerly, for I was always ready to talk about my pet.
