I said, a little timidly, “why your cat grins like that?”
“It’s a Cheshire cat,”
 the Duchess replied, “and that’s why. Pig!”
Her sudden outburst made me jump, but I realized she was addressing the baby, not me.
 Taking courage, I continued, “I didn’t know that Cheshire cats always grinned; in fact, I didn’t know that cats _could_ grin.”
“They all can,”
 the Duchess said, “and most of ’em do.”
