“You _must_ remember,”
 the King insisted, “or I’ll have you executed.”
The miserable Hatter dropped his teacup and bread-and-butter, going down on one knee.
 “I’m a poor man, your Majesty,”
 he began.
“You’re a _very_ poor _speaker_,”
 the King declared.
One of the guinea-pigs cheered, but was immediately suppressed by the officers of the court.
