I do wish they _would_ put their heads down!
 I am so _very_ tired of being all alone here!
”As I said this, I looked down at my hands, and was surprised to see that I had put on one of the Rabbit’s little white kid gloves while I was talking. “
How _can_ I have done that?
” I thought. “
I must be growing small again.
” I got up and went to the table to measure myself by it, and found that, as nearly as I could guess, I was now about two feet high, and was going on shrinking rapidly: I soon found out that the cause of this was the fan I was holding, and I dropped it hastily, just in time to avoid shrinking away altogether.“
That _was_ a narrow escape!
