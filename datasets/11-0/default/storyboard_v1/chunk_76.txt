I hastily replied;
 “only one doesn’t like changing so often, you know.”
 “I _don’t_ know,”
 said the Caterpillar.
I said nothing: I had never been so much contradicted in my life before, and I felt that I was losing my temper.
 “Are you content now?”
 said the Caterpillar.
 “Well, I should like to be a _little_ larger, sir, if you wouldn’t mind,”
