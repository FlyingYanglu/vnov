“If everybody minded their own business,”
 the Duchess said in a hoarse growl, “the world would go round a deal faster than it does.”
“Which would _not_ be an advantage,”
 I said, glad to show off a little of my knowledge.
 “Just think of what work it would make with the day and night! You see the earth takes twenty-four hours to turn round on its axis—”
