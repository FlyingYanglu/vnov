I’ll fetch the executioner myself,” the King eagerly said, and he hurried off.I thought I might as well go back, and see how the game was going on, as I heard the Queen’s voice in the distance, screaming with passion. I had already heard her sentence three of the players to be executed for having missed their turns, and I didn’t like the look of things at all, as the game was in such confusion that I never knew whether it was my turn or not. So I went in search of my hedgehog.The hedgehog was engaged in a fight with another hedgehog, which seemed to me an excellent opportunity for croqueting one of them with the other: the only difficulty was, that my flamingo was gone across to the other side of the garden, where I could see it trying in a helpless sort of way to fly up into a tree.By the time I had caught the flamingo and brought it back, the fight was over, and both the hedgehogs were out of sight: “

The Queen’s argument was, that if something wasn’t done about it in less than no time she’d have everybody executed, all round. This last remark made everyone look so grave and anxious.
