I sighed wearily.
 "I think you might do something better with the time,"
 I said, "than waste it in asking riddles that have no answers."
 "If you knew Time as well as I do,"
 said the Hatter, "you wouldn’t talk about wasting _it_. It’s _him_."
 "I don’t know what you mean,"
 I said.
 "Of course you don’t!"
