“So you did, old fellow!”
 said the others.
“We must burn the house down!”
 said the Rabbit’s voice;
 and I called out as loud as I could, “If you do, I’ll set Dinah at you!”
There was a dead silence instantly, and I thought to myself, “I wonder what they will do next! If they had any sense, they’d take the roof off.”
 After a minute or two, they began moving about again, and I heard the Rabbit say, “A barrowful will do, to begin with.”
“A barrowful of what?”
