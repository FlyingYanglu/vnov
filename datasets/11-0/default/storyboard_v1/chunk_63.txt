Oh dear!
 I’d nearly forgotten that I’ve got to grow up again!
 Let me see—how _is_ it to be managed?
 I suppose I ought to eat or drink something or other;
 but the great question is, what?
The great question certainly was, what?
 I looked all around me at the flowers and the blades of grass, but I did not see anything that looked like the right thing to eat or drink under the circumstances.
