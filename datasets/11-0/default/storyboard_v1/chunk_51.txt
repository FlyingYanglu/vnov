“Oh, you foolish Alice!”
 I answered myself.
 “How can you learn lessons in here? Why, there’s hardly room for _you_, and no room at all for any lesson-books!”
 And so I went on, taking first one side and then the other, and making quite a conversation of it altogether;
 but after a few minutes I heard a voice outside, and stopped to listen.
“Mary Ann! Mary Ann!”
 said the voice.
 “Fetch me my gloves this moment!”
