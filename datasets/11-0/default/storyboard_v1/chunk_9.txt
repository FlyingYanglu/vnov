However, this bottle was not marked "poison,"
 so I ventured to taste it, and finding it very nice, (it had, in fact, a sort of mixed flavour of cherry-tart, custard, pine-apple, roast turkey, toffee, and hot buttered toast,) I very soon finished it off.
"What a curious feeling!"
 I said;
 "I must be shutting up like a telescope."
 And so it was indeed: I was now only ten inches high, and my face brightened up at the thought that I was now the right size for going through the little door into that lovely garden.
 First, however, I waited for a few minutes to see if I was going to shrink any further.
